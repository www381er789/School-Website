Established in 1998, Rustomjee International School has built a formidable reputation in the field of education, with our students having excelled in academics, sports and extra-curricular activities. We aim to ignite the spirit, liberate the creativity, and then let the patterns emerge in different shapes and forms. We endeavour to help our children stretch their boundaries and discover their hidden talents, to encourage them to stand up every time they fall, to teach them not to turn away from their responsibilities, and to instil in them the confidence to stand up for their principles even if they stand alone. Our children imbibe that a winner is the one who has the ability to overcome all difficulties and setbacks, and come back with even better resolve.

    SCHOOL CANTEEN
    School canteen facility aims to provide children of working parents to buy nutritious and healthy food at competitive prices during the school day. Meals are either cooked on the premises or bought from approved restaurants and food outlets. Pupils who cannot take full breakfast from home may bring some form of healthy ‘snack’ with them for break time. Pupils can also buy snacks items and fruits from the snacks counter. Parents shall send canteen food order slip or coupon through the pupil for availing canteen services. Payments can be made daily, weekly or monthly. There is a choice of menu that operates on a rota basis during the week. If you would like to learn more about the menu and payment system, please contact the Catering Supervisor. Parents are requested to consider healthy food options with quantity appropriate to the age and appetite of the student. Pupils are encouraged to bring and use fork and spoon for their lunch. Parents are advised to send packed lunches covered with a towel. Pupils may bring water to drink during the day in a clean water bottle. Water dispensers are provided for them to refill their bottles during the day. ​ Fizzy drinks, chewing/bubble gums, etc., are not to be brought to school at anytime.
